Wesley Teal

Iowa State University Library

Avian Archives of Iowa Online (avIAn)

https://avian.lib.iastate.edu/

  • Web portal for ornithological primary sources from Iowa
  • Coverage from 1819 to 2011
  • 8 physical collections
  • ~ 10,000 digital objects
  • Photographs, videos, lantern slides, letters, rare bird reports, and more!

Project overview

  • June 2017 - June 2019
  • Funded in part by a Digitizing Hidden Collections grant from CLIR
  • Collaboration between 26 staff members and 5 student employees
    • 2 staff members hired specifically for project

Data in the documents

IOU rare bird occurrences

  • ~ 2,500 well-documented rare bird occurrences
  • 194 species, plus 5 at genera- or family-level
  • ~ 670 locations

Darwin Core

Darwin Core is a standard glossary of terms intented to "facilitate the sharing of information about biological diversity"1.

1. https://www.tdwg.org/standards/dwc/.
